Local Running Club - Site Plan

Site Name

Running Club Cuautla

Cuautla is the small town where I'm from. This name was chosen to represent the community. It's simple, descriptive and emphasizes the location to attract runners from the area.

Domain: runningclub-c.mx (WIP)

Site Purpose

The website connects runners in the local area, promotes healthy habits, and helps people find training sessions, running routes, and join the club.

Scenarios

  • Are there people who share my hobby for running near me?
  • How do I join the club or ask questions?
